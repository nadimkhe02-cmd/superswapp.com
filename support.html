Frequently Asked Questions

What is SuperSwap?

SuperSwap is a crew scheduling coordination tool that allows airline flight crew to view, request, and manage shift swaps with colleagues.

Who can use SuperSwap?

SuperSwap is designed for airline crew members including Captains, First Officers, and scheduling administrators within their organisation.

How do I log in?

Use the credentials provided by your company administrator. If you're having trouble logging in, contact your admin or reach out to our support email.

Is the app free?

Yes, SuperSwap is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases or subscription fees.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to nadim@superswapp.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS/Android version.
