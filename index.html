FAQ

What is SuperSwap?

SuperSwap is a scheduling coordination tool that allows users to view, request, and manage shift swaps easily.

Who can use SuperSwap?

Anyone who needs to coordinate and swap shifts with their team can use SuperSwap.

How do I log in?

Log in using the email and password you registered with. If you're having trouble, reach out to us below and we'll help you get back in.

Is the app free?

Yes, SuperSwap is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases or subscription fees.
